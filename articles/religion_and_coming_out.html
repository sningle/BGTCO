What do I do if other people don’t accept me because of their religion?

Although it may be difficult to deal with others who don’t accept your identity for religious reasons, there are multiple ways to go about trying to come out to or interact with those people.

Some people may choose to completely stop interacting with religious people who do not accept the LGBTQ+ community for their own well-being. Others may try to help those people understand their perspective and come to accept queer people.

For this, one might be interested in The Reformation Project, The Religious Institute, or the Human Rights Campaign’s info on debunking homophobia in Christianity.
